Your hand hovers over the controls.

The machine reacts instantly — lights surge, the room bends, the air grows heavier, and a weight settles in your chest.

Something has already changed.

Where do you go?

  • Step backward — into the past. You have another chance to protect your friend before everything collapses.
  • Step forward — into the future. You meet your older self, who is at peace with herself and her mistakes.
  • Hesitate — your hand slips across the controls. A bright light pierces the room, pushing you to the floor.
